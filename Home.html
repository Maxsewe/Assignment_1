Mama Mboga Grocery is an in-store shopping vendor and delivery grocery located in Embakasi, Outering road, Fedha.

The grocery open from 8:00am to 10:00pm every day except on Sundays that it opens at noon (12:00pm) to 10pm.

What makes the grocery exquisitely popular is its affordable package prices that make shopping easier and its flexibility to order online resulting to market credibility and reliability.

Our customers are our heart and soul
