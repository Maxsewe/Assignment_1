Mama Mboga Grocery started in 2021 just as an idea of retailing surplus from the domestic farm produce. Since then, it has grown to be a grocery vendor offering organic products. Mama mboga deals with fruits, vegetables, cereals and pantry items

The fundamental mission statement is "go green for a healthy life", true to their words it's the life our ancestors dreamed of

Customers love for the products is because everything is organic, non-GMO

Mission

To raise healthy generations with greens

Vision

To have branch outlets across the country and a standardized online hyper market

Our customers are our heart and soul
